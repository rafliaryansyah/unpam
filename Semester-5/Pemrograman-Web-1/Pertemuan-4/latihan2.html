Menampilkan Gambar, Suara & Video

Berikut contoh implementasi multimedia dalam HTML:

Gambar (Image)

<img src="..." alt="..." width="250" height="180">

Audio

<audio controls><source src="..."></audio>

Video

<video width="400" height="225" controls><source src="..."></video>

Atribut Multimedia

Tag Atribut Penting Keterangan
<img> src, alt, width, height Menampilkan gambar
<audio> controls, autoplay, loop Memutar file audio
<video> controls, width, height, autoplay Memutar file video
<source> src, type Sumber media alternatif
